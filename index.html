🏆 Today's BTS Pick — Fri Mar 27
RONALD ACUÑA JR.
RF · Atlanta Braves · vs. Cole Ragans (LHP) · 7:15 PM ET · Truist Park · 87°F
This is the clearest BTS play of Opening Week. Acuña returned from his second ACL surgery in 2025 and looked every bit the MVP he was in 2023 — posting a .403 wOBA, 92.7 avg exit velocity, and 52.5% hard-hit rate in 95 games. He leads off, meaning 3–4 guaranteed plate appearances. Cole Ragans is a deceptive matchup — his 4.67 ERA in 2025 was one of the most misleading numbers in baseball. For BTS purposes, that's exactly what we want — a pitcher who gives up contact, facing one of the most elite contact-quality hitters alive. Add in 87°F heat at Truist Park's 1,050 ft elevation — the hottest conditions on today's entire slate — and Acuña is the most compelling pick on the board today.
